Hi there, I'm Tabitha Obara

‘To design is much more than simply to assemble, to order, or even to edit; it is to add value and meaning, to illuminate, to simplify, to clarify, to modify, to dignify, to dramatize, to persuade, and perhaps even to amuse. To design is to transform prose into poetry. Design broadens perception, magnifies experience, and enhances vision. Design is the product of feeling and awareness, of ideas that originate in the mind of the designer and culminate, one hopes, in the mind of the spectator.’

Paul Rand, Design form and chaos, 1993

About Me

With 7 years’ experience in graphic, web design and photography as a graphic designer and creative lead for advertising agencies, international non-governmental organizations and later a freelancer on wide range of multi-faceted projects. I explore functionality of design for visual communication and impact in all my work, with a love for communication for development. I believe that every piece of design should always invoke the intended emotion despite being a work of art.
